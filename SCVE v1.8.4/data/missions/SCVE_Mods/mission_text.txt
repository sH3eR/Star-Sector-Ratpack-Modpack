List of mod ships, sorted by tech type, size and DP.

Hold LEFT/RIGHT ARROW while clicking on the mission to switch through the different mods.

Hold Q or W while clicking on the mission to toggle between hiding ships:
    - heavy filter (removes HIDE_IN_CODEX) /
    - light filter (removes restricted) /
    - no filter

Hold A or S while clicking on the mission to toggle between hiding weapons/wings:
    - heavy filter (only this mod's weapons/wings) /
    - light filter (vanilla + this mod's weapons/wings) /
    - default (all mod weapons/wings + vanilla) /
    - no filter (including restricted weapons (wings currently glitchy))
	- custom filter (defined in custom_<weapon/wing>_filter.csv)

Hold Z or X while clicking on the mission to toggle showing/hiding additional hullmods:
    - default hullmods only /
    - show add/remove perma-mod hullmods /
    - show d-mods /
    - show all hidden mods

Hold SPACE while clicking on the mission to restore default weapon/wing/hullmod availability.

You can find .variant files for the ships you edit in Starsector/saves/missions/SCVE_Mods.
